# Burger-Stack-Frenzy
Is a game where your objective is to make burgers for customers by stacking each ingredient on a plate in the right order. You have 10 minutes to complete as many orders as possbile and get the most points. in order to complete orders you have to find ingredients, quarter them using a knive then cook the burger, assemble the patties, and finally hit the order bell. Be careful not to drop any food as there are plenty of rats that will take the food back to their home. You can stop the rats by shooting them with a gun which will prevent them from coming back in the future.
